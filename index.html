Icicles of The Apostle Islands

(A Spin-Off of Towers of Hanoi)

Rules: 1. Only one disk can be moved at a time (double-click to select, single click to move). 2. Each move consists of taking the upper disk from one of the stacks and placing it on top of another stack. 3. No disk may be placed on top of a smaller disk. 4. Get all the disks from the first cell into the last. Good Luck!
